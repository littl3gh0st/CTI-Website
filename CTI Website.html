Threat Actor Overview

DeceptiveDevelopment, also associated with WageMole and Contagious Interview campaigns, is a financially motivated, North Korea-aligned threat group. They execute sophisticated social engineering campaigns targeting software developers to steal cryptocurrency and fraudulently obtain remote employment.

Primary Motivation

Financial Gain (Crypto Theft)

Primary Targets

Software & Web3 Developers

Targeted Platforms

Windows, macOS, and Linux

Key Deception Tactic

Fake Job Recruiters
